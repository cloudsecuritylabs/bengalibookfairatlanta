The Event

Atlanta Bangla Boi Mela 2026

✦

Welcome to the Atlanta Bengali community's most anticipated cultural celebration of 2026. The Poem Exhibition + Bengali Book Fair of Local Authors brings together poets, artists, dancers, reciters, and readers under one roof in Cumming, Georgia — a gathering that honors the profound literary and artistic heritage of Bengal.

Selected poems by our featured poets have been reimagined as paintings by featured visual artists, creating a unique dialogue between the written word and the visual image. Invited reciters will lend new dimensions to these poems through live performance, breathing fresh life into each verse.

The event also features a Bengali Book Fair where you can browse and purchase works by local authors, with books and paintings available for pre-order and collection on the day. All are welcome — entry is completely free.

All local authors are warmly invited to participate in the book fair. Join us in celebrating the enduring power of Bengali language, poetry, and art.

✦ Poem Exhibition

Original poems by featured poets transformed into paintings by featured visual artists — a rare cross-medium artistic experience.

✦ Bengali Book Fair

Browse and purchase books by local Bengali authors. Pre-order now and collect at the event. All local authors are invited to participate.

✦ Live Poetry Recitation

Invited reciters perform selected poems, offering fresh interpretations that elevate the words of our featured poets.

✦ Album Releases

Two new albums released live at the event — Rabindrasangeet by Shrabanti Basu and Baul songs by Tarun Khyapa.

✦ Charity

50% of all proceeds go to St. Jude Children's Research Hospital. Every purchase supports both the artists and a vital cause.
